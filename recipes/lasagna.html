Lasagna

Lasagna is a classic italian dish made with layers of pasta, a rich meat or vegetable sauce and various cheeses. It is a hearty and comforting meal, perfect for family dinners or special occasions.

Ingredients

  • Lasagna Noodles
  • Meat Sauce
  • Cheese
  • Bechamel Sauce(optional but traditional)

Preparation Steps

  1. Prepare the sauces
  2. Cook the Noodles
  3. Assemble the lasagna
  4. Layer the fillings
  5. Repeat Layes
  6. Bake
  7. Rest and Serve
